ABOUT THE MALDIVES

THE ISLAND EXPERIENCE

The Maldives cosists of a nation of 1192 islands that span a distance of 90,000 kilometres square, only 298 square kilometres are comprised of dry land! The islands are a group of 2 chains, and 26 atolls, only 200 of these are inhabited! The Maldive's natural beauty is not only visible on dry land, it's splendid natural wonder is the coral reef systems, making up about 5 percent of the planet's coral reefs.
